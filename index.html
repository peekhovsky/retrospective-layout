And here we go again!

On March 29 and 30, a retrospective of student cinema will be held at the European Humanitarian Universify for the 9th lime. The theme for this year's shows is (Re) thinking cinema. You can send video clips made as part of training courses and your personal work. Do not be afraid to experiment!

We will be waiting for work that you would like to share at cinernaehu@gmail.com. Upload the video fo the file sharing service (dropbox, we+ransfer, efc.) and copy the link. Do not forget to specify in the letter the name, duration, genre and authors of the video, as well as a short (or long, if you are an artist and you see so) a description of the work.

We, like all students, do not like the word "deadline", but in order for everything to happen, we ask you to send your applications by March 251h.
